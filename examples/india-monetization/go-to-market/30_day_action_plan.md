# 30-Day Action Plan for India Market Launch

**Objective:** Generate first ₹10 lakhs revenue in 90 days  
**Focus:** CA/CS firms (fastest revenue) + TReDS platforms

## Week 1: Foundation & Registration

### Day 1-2: Government Registration Setup
- [ ] **DPIIT Startup Recognition** (Free, mandatory)
  - Visit: https://www.startupindia.gov.in/
  - Documents: Incorporation certificate, PAN, description of innovation
  - Benefits: Tax exemption, credibility, GeM access
  - **Time:** 2-3 hours online form
  - **Cost:** ₹0 (government program)

- [ ] **GeM (Government e-Marketplace) Registration** (Free)
  - Visit: https://gem.gov.in/
  - Required for: All government sales (₹4+ lakh Cr marketplace)
  - Documents: PAN, GST, bank details, DPIIT certificate
  - **Time:** 1 day
  - **Cost:** ₹0

### Day 3-4: Industry Association Memberships
- [ ] **NASSCOM Membership** (₹50,000/year)
  - Benefits: Networking, events, credibility
  - Contact: membership@nasscom.in
  - ROI: Access to 2,000+ member companies

- [ ] **CII/FICCI Membership** (₹25,000-1 lakh/year)
  - Benefits: Government connect, industry events
  - Choose: CII for manufacturing, FICCI for services
  - ROI: Government tender visibility

### Day 5-7: CA/CS Market Research
- [ ] **Scrape ICAI Directory**
  - Target: 100 medium firms (20-100 CAs) in Bangalore/Mumbai
  - Data needed: Firm name, managing partner, LinkedIn profile
  - Tool: Manual + LinkedIn Sales Navigator (₹10,000/month)

- [ ] **Competitive Analysis**
  - Try: Taxmann, CCH, Tally (free trials)
  - Document: Features, pricing, gaps
  - Position: Sutra's unique value (temporal + causal reasoning)

**Week 1 Deliverables:**
- ✅ DPIIT recognition certificate
- ✅ GeM registration complete
- ✅ NASSCOM membership active
- ✅ 100 target CA firms identified

**Budget Spent:** ₹75,000 (memberships + tools)

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Demo Conversion (<50%)
**Mitigation:** 
- Improve demo script based on feedback
- Add customer testimonials
- Offer longer trial (3 months)

### Risk 2: Trial Firms Don't Use Product
**Mitigation:**
- Daily usage reminders via WhatsApp
- Weekly check-in calls
- Gamification (query count leaderboard)

### Risk 3: TReDS Platforms Slow to Respond
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on CA/CS market (faster sales cycle)
- Use TReDS success as case study for banks later

### Risk 4: Budget Overrun
**Mitigation:**
- NASSCOM/CII membership optional (defer if needed)
- Use free tools (OBS instead of Loom, manual instead of Mailchimp)
- **Core budget:** ₹50,000 (NASSCOM + tools), rest optional

## Key Learnings to Track

### What to Measure
1. **Email metrics:** Open rate, reply rate, demo booking rate
2. **Demo metrics:** Show rate, trial conversion rate
3. **Trial metrics:** Daily active users, query volume, satisfaction
4. **Conversion metrics:** Trial to paid conversion, ACV, payback period

### What to Iterate
1. **Email subject lines** (optimize for >25% open rate)
2. **Demo script** (optimize for >50% trial conversion)
3. **Product features** (based on trial feedback)
4. **Pricing** (test monthly vs annual, discount levels)
